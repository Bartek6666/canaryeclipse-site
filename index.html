See the 12 August 2026 eclipse from the Canaries

Free Android app · Partial solar eclipse · 12 Aug 2026, ~19:53 WEST · Canary Islands

On 12 August 2026 the Sun sets partly eclipsed over the Canary Islands — up to ~78% covered, very low on the horizon. Exactly where and when to look changes from island to island. This free app works it out for your spot.

What it does

  • Exact eclipse times (first contact, maximum, sunset) for any point on the islands
  • Sun altitude & azimuth at maximum — so you know where to aim
  • AR sun-finder: point your phone and line up where the Sun will be
  • Calima (Saharan dust) outlook and a clear-horizon view-quality score
  • Works for curated viewpoints, Google Places search, or your live GPS
